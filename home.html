Who are we?

Tufts Alpaca Farm is a Tufts University sponsored, student run, farm that helps take care of alpacas. We also offer tours and teach about these interesting and adorable animals, as well as sell alpaca products. Consider booking a reservation, whether just for the family, or for even small events like parties and birthdays!


New Event: Alpaca Therapy

We are pleased to announce that we are now offering alpaca-assisted therapeutic activities on our ranch. If you are interested in learning more about animal assisted therapy and how alpacas may help you, please contact us at tuftsalpacafarm@tufts.edu or through the contact page on this site.


HIRING: Assistant Shearer

We are currently seeking a motivated and skilled Assistant Shearer to join our team. As an Assistant Shearer, you will work closely with our experienced shearers to ensure the efficient and humane shearing of our sheep. This is an excellent opportunity for individuals passionate about animal care and interested in contributing to the wool production process.

Key Responsibilities include:

  • Assist in preparing sheep for shearing, including sorting, restraining, and cleaning animals.
  • Operate shearing equipment under the guidance of experienced shearers.
  • Handling sheep with care and ensure their safety and comfort throughout the shearing process.
  • Assist in maintaining a clean and organized work environment.
  • Follow all safety protocols and guidelines to prevent accidents and ensure a safe working environment for all team members.

If you are passionate about animal care and interested in joining our team as an Assistant Shearer, please submit your resume and a brief cover letter outlining your relevant experience and why you are interested in this position. We look forward to hearing from you! Please submit your application via email to tuftsalpacafarm@tufts.edu with the subject line "Assistant Shearer Application - [Your Name]." Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis until the position is filled.
